Third Section

The box model is an important core concept of cascading style sheets because every HTML element is basically a box made up of content, padding, border and margin. Below are the differences between padding, margin and border

Padding: This is the part of the box that is between the content and the border

Margin: This is the immediate entire space around the box.

Border: This is the edge of the box, the part of the box that is between the padding and margin.
